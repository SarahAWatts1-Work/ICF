About BDS24 Architect and Your Program Facilitator

Dubbed the personal branding guru by Entrepreneur magazine, William Arruda is a branding expert, consultant, keynote speaker and trainer. His innovative work has transformed personal branding into a global industry, thanks to his revolutionary three-step branding methodology that has empowered hundreds of thousands of professionals across the globe.

William’s expertise has been sought after by CEOs, thought-leaders and career-driven professionals. He has worked with thousands of coaches to help them build successful practices that are aligned with their values and purpose. His corporate clients include many of the world’s most revered brands, including, Adobe, BMW, Google, Gucci, J&J, LinkedIn, L'Oréal, Microsoft, and Warner Media.

William is the author of the definitive books on personal branding, Career Distinction, Ditch. Dare. Do! and Digital YOU, and he is a senior contributor to Forbes where his column has amassed over ten million views. His thought leadership and influence in the domain have earned him the title of the #1 Brand Guru by Global Gurus in 2022 and 2023. He also received the Chair Award from the ICF for his contribution to the field of coaching.

In collaboration with the ICF, William co-created the Business Development Series a decade ago, driven by a mutual commitment to help coaches elevate their success. This biennial program, rooted in William's proven three-step process, is consistently updated to equip coaches with the latest insights, tools, and resources to achieve their professional goals.
